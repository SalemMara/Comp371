Movement of Model:
D -> Translate Right
A -> Translate Left
W -> Translate Up
S -> Translate Down
Spacebar -> Position you to a random spot in the grid

Scale of Model:
Z -> Scale up
X -> Scale down

Rotation of whole Model Y-axis:
R -> Rotate to the right
F -> Rotate to the left

Rotation of Hand + Raquet along Y-axis:
M -> Rotate to the right
N -> Rotate to the left

Movement of Camera:
Left arrow -> Camera moves to the left
Right arrow -> Camera moves to the right
Up arrow -> Camera moves up
Down arrow -> Camera moves down
Home -> Bring you to initial position of camera

Zooming:
J -> Zoom Out
U -> Zoom In
